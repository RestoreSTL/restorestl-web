How it works

The process from start to payoff

We find a property

Our team identifies a distressed property in St. Louis with strong profit potential and presents you the deal.

You review the numbers

Purchase price, repair costs, after-repair value, your loan amount, and your expected return. All transparent.

You fund through the title company

Your money goes directly to the title company — not to us. They handle the closing, just like any real estate transaction.

Your lien is recorded

A deed of trust is recorded in your name. You are in 1st position on the property. This is your security.

We do the work

We renovate and sell the property, or assign the contract to another buyer. You get photo updates throughout.

You get paid at closing

When the property sells, the title company pays you first — your principal plus 10% annualized interest. No monthly payments to track.

Honest risk disclosure

What could go wrong — and how you’re protected

“What if the property doesn’t sell?”
You hold a 1st position lien. If we can’t sell, you can take ownership of the property. You’re never left empty-handed.
“What if repairs cost more than expected?”
That’s our risk, not yours. Your loan amount is fixed. Overruns come out of our profit, not your return.
“What if it takes longer than expected?”
Your interest keeps accruing. A longer hold means you earn more interest, not less. You get paid when it closes.
“Where does my money actually go?”
Directly to the title company. They handle the disbursement at closing. They also pay you directly when the deal is done.
